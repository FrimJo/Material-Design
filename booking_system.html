Summary

This is a school project where a group of six students where to find a company and do some kind of design, build and test towards and in collaboration with them.


Our company needed a more intuitive way of booking their group rooms and a easier way to see which are available or when they will be free for use.


At the moment they used Office 365 and hade a resource for each room which they used when booking a meeting.


We decided to develop a web application which will be run on surfpads at each group room. Some criterias of the applikation where:


  • You should be able to clearly see if a group room is, at the moment, occupied or available.

  • You should clearly see the next available time.

  • Should be easy to create a meeting at and with a specifik group room.

  • You should be able to create a meeting in the current day.

  • The application needs to be fully functional on both android and iOS.
